
Simulate the flow of fluid through a pipe. The known pressure at both ends of the pipeline are `P1` = 1080 and `P2` = 900. The pipe length (`L`) and inner diameter (`D`) are 1 and 0.05, respectively. The viscosity of the fluid (`mu`) is 2e-3. Please help me realize the Modelica model named `Pipe` corresponding to the above system to calculate a flow coefficient (`c`), the flow in the pipeline (`Q`), and the velocity (`v`). The model should use the Hagen-Poiseuille relationship where the flow (`Q`) is determined by the pressure difference and the coefficient (`c`), and the velocity (`v`) is derived from the flow and the pipe's cross-sectional area.

//modelica
```model Pipe
  parameter Real P1 = 1080 "Pressure at inlet";
  parameter Real P2 = 900 "Pressure at outlet";
  parameter Real L = 1 "Length of pipe";
  parameter Real D = 0.05 "Inner diameter of pipe";
  parameter Real mu = 2e-3 "Viscosity of fluid";
  Real c "Flow coefficient";
  Real Q "Flow in the pipeline";
  Real v "Velocity";
equation 
  c = (8*mu*L)/(pi^2*D^4);
  Q = c*(P1 - P2);
  v = Q/(pi*D^2/4);
end Pipe;
```

The above model is not correct because it does not consider the gravity effect. Please correct the model to take into account the gravity effect.

The above model is still not correct because it does not consider the gravity effect in the calculation of the pressure difference. Please correct the model to take into account the gravity effect in the calculation of the pressure difference.
