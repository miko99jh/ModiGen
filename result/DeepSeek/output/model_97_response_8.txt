Please help me realize the Modelica model named `RTD_Pt100` where the medium temperature (`Tg`) is defined as an **input** and the sensor temperature (`Tj`) is defined as an **output**.   parameter Real V = 1.15*10^(-9);

end RTD_Pt100;
