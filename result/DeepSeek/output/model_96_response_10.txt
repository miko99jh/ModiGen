The Hagen-Poiseuille equation is given by:

```
Q = c * (P1 - P2)
```

where `c` is the flow coefficient and `P1` and `P2` are the pressures at the two ends of the pipe.

The velocity `v` is given by:

```
v = Q / (pi * D^2 / 4)
```

where `Q` is the flow, `D` is the diameter of the pipe, and `pi` is the mathematical constant Pi.

The flow coefficient `c` can be calculated from the Hagen-Poiseuille equation as:

```
c = (8 * mu * L) / (pi * D^4)
```

where `mu` is the dynamic viscosity of the fluid, `L` is the length of the pipe, and `D` is the diameter of the pipe.

model Pipe
