::

Partial model of field circuit of asynchronous induction machines

Information
-----------

::

This model is used to model the field circuit of an asynchronous
induction machine. It is a partial model and can be used to model the
field circuit of an asynchronous induction machine with different types
of saturation and losses.

::

Extends from
    Modelica.Electrical.Machines.Interfaces.PartialFieldCircuit (Partial model of field circuit of asynchronous induction machines).
