
Suppose we have a hydraulic system consisting of two oil storage tanks and a cylindrical connecting pipe. The connection pipe has a length (`L`) of 0.1 and a diameter (`D`) of 0.2, and the dynamic viscosity (`mu`) of the oil is 2e-3. The model also calculates a flow coefficient (`c`) based on the pipe dimensions and viscosity. Please help me implement the modelica model named `TwoTanks` of this system, including equations for pressures (`P1`, `P2`), flow rate (`Q`), and the conservation of mass to determine the rate of change of the heights.
