```modelica
model AmbientCondition
  parameter Modelica.SIunits.Temperature T_amb = 293.15 "Ambient temperature in Kelvin";
  
  Modelica.Thermal.HeatTransfer.Interfaces.HeatPort_a node "Heat transfer interface";

equation
```

- **Equation `node.T = T_amb`:** This equation ensures that the temperature at the heat port is always equal to the ambient temperature.
- **Equation `node.Q_flow = 0`:** This equation ensures that there is no heat flow into or out of the infinite reservoir, representing its infinite capacity to absorb or supply heat without changing temperature.
