This Modelica model defines an ideal operational amplifier with three pins: two input pins (`in_p` and `in_n`) and one output pin (`out`). The model enforces the ideal behavior of an operational amplifier by ensuring that the voltages at the input pins are equal and that the currents at the input pins are zero. The output pin is modeled as a norator, which means it can have any voltage and current, representing the ideal behavior of an operational amplifier's output.
