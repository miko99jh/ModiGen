This Modelica model defines a `CellStack` that extends the `BaseCellStack` from the Modelica Electrical Batteries library. It sets the inner resistance `R` based on the number of series (`Ns`) and parallel (`Np`) cells and the inner resistance of a single cell (`cellData.Ri`). The `cellData` parameter is redeclared to use the `CellData` record from the same library. The connection between `r0.n` and `n` is established as specified. Additional parameters and equations can be added to model the open-circuit voltage, self-discharge, and other battery characteristics.
