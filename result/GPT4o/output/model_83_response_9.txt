```modelica
model Short
  extends Modelica.Electrical.Polyphase.Interfaces.TwoPlug;

  parameter Integer m = 3 "Number of phases";
  Modelica.Electrical.Analog.Ideal.Short short[m];

equation
  for i in 1:m loop
    connect(plug_p.pin[i], short[i].p);
    connect(short[i].n, plug_n.pin[i]);
  end for;
end Short;
```

This Modelica model named `Short` represents a polyphase short circuit branch. It extends from `Modelica.Electrical.Polyphase.Interfaces.TwoPlug` and includes an array of ideal short circuits. The model establishes connections between the polyphase plugs and the short circuit array to create a direct electrical bridge.
